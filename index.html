About me


My name is Humberto Pontes. I was born in 1991, in Braga, Portugal, and moved with my family to Marseille, France, three years later. I lived in France up to the beginning of my adult life. There, I graduated as a bachelor in management and as a master in finance, from Aix-Marseille II University. During my master’s degree, I had an enriching experience advising companies on investment strategies, in Provence Création’s enterprises.


In 2017, I decided to return to Portugal and open my own financial consulting company. I, then, advised companies on matters such as investment strategies, auditing, taxation, cost reduction, and increase in profitability. I worked with several companies from different sectors, for example clothing, food, distribution, energy, finance, chemicals, and transport.


However, I have always had a deeper interest for working with financial investments. So, I decided to focus my work in that area. Directing my work to support individuals and their financial management is something that deeply stimulates me and speak to my core values.


Currently, I am living in Espoo, a growing city of 270,000 inhabitants that belongs to the Helsinki metropolitan area, in Finland. The decision to base my company here was not a difficult one since I strongly identify with the Nordic culture.


Most of the companies I work with are located in the United States of America. I also work with clients from all across Europe, such as Portugal, Spain, France, Italy, Germany, and the United Kingdom.

How do you invest?


First, you can contact me by e-mail or phone call and we will arrange an appointment, either online or in person. I will answer all your questions and we will go through the terms of possible investments for you.


Second, you will decide on the amount you would like to invest and, after that, you will receive a contract by mail or e-mail. After we both agree to and sign the contract, your investment will start.


One month before your investment completes one year, you will receive a message from me, containing the information of the investment. There, you will inform me whether you want to keep investing the full amount, retrieve the full amount, or retrieve the profits.
