About me



I am Humberto, Portuguese, grew up in France, born in 1991. I studied a bachelor's degree in management (AES companies management), and a master's degree in Finance at Aix-Marseille II University. During the master's degree, I did a curricular internship for six months, in Provence Création d'entreprises. My role was to advise companies on investment strategies.


During three years, I had a financial consulting company in Portugal. I have advised companies on investment strategies, auditing, cost reduction, increase in profitability, increase in profit, taxation. I have worked with several companies from different sectors, clothing, food, distribution, energy, finance, chemicals, transport, industry. Mostly companies are located in the United States of America, but also in Portugal, Spain, France, Italy, Germany, United Kingdom.


My ambition has always been to work in financial investments, so I decided to focus my work on financial investments to the detriment of business consulting, for two reasons:

  • First of all, I believe that financial investments are more stimulating.

  • Then being able to work mostly for individuals and help them in their financial management correspond more to my values.


Currently, I am living in Espoo, Finland. My company "Pontes Investments" is based here. I can work with people all over the world, my work has no borders. I chose to move to Finland because I identify with Nordic culture.
