Wealth Management

I can help you manage your wealth, money, or assets. I will show you different options for management, depending on your profile and goals, and together we will choose what is the best option for you. Usually, there are two options for wealth management: the one with no risk and less profit, or the one with existing risk but more profit. In sum, the risk-reward ratio is proportional: if there is less risk, the return is lower, and vice-versa.


I can also support you in managing your monthly budget. In this case, we will together analyze your income and expenses. We will discuss about your monetary and personal goals and how to achieve them with lower costs, according to your own needs, towards a peaceful end of the month.

Investments

If you decide to invest with me, my work is to invest your money. The investment will bring you a net profit of 10 percent a year. This financial investment is flexible, because you have the possibility to withdraw your investment at any time, without paying penalties. However, to collect the interest your investment generated, it must be maintained for a minimum of one year. After that, you have the option of withdrawing or leaving your profits within the financial investment, to generate more profits.


When I invest your money for you, it is placed in strategic companies and projects, with good economic prospects. It is a personal choice of mine not to invest your money in the stock market, because these investments offer more risks. As a professional, I prefer to promise my clients less profit and ensure absence of risks. All investments and related profits entrusted with me are protected by insurance.

Financial Advisor

This service is intended for companies.


Here, I give you advice to improve your company’s profits and lower your operating costs, help you define an investment strategy, advise in financial audits and conduct market studies. These options are independent of each other. You can choose to purchase one or more of them. The central idea is to offer a personalized service adapted to each situation, taking into account the specificity of the company, the sector of activity, the micro and macroeconomic situation, as well as local demand.

Ethics charter

I promise not to invest your money in companies that employ minors under the age of 14, using resources from an armed conflict, do not respect Human Rights, women's rights, finance criminal organizations or not fully transparent.


I prioritize companies that promote gender equality, transparency, reliability in commitments, respect the law, have long-term vision, and good resource management.
