Wealth Management

Investments

My work is to invest your money, the investment will bring you a net profit of 10% per year. It is flexible for you because you can get your money anytime. However, if your money is invested less than one year, you do not receive the benefits.


To guarantee your investment, I have insurance that protects the total amount invested as well as the benefits associated with the investment.


After each year of investment, you can get back the full amount, just the profits, or continue to invest the full amount.
