Wealth Management

If you have a lot of money, I can help you manage your money, wealth, or assets. I show you different options for management, and together we choose what is the best option for you.


Usually there are two options for wealth management, there is management without risk but less profitable, or management with existing risk but more profitable. The risk-reward ratio is proportional if there is less risk the return is lower and vice versa.


If you need help to manage your monthly budget, we will look at your income, bills. We will see with you what your monetary and personal goals are. We will work to lower costs, according to the needs for a peaceful end of the month.

Investments

My work is to invest your money, the investment will bring you a net profit of 10% per year. This financial investment is flexible because you have the possibility to withdraw your investment at any time without paying penalties. To receive your interest your investment must last a minimum of one year. You have the option of withdrawing or leaving your profits within the financial investment.


The money invested is placed in strategic companies, projects with good economic prospects. I have chosen not to invest your money in the stock market because these investments are risky and I have chosen not to take risks with the money entrusted to me. I prefer to promise less profits but not to take risks. The investment and the related profits are protected by insurance.

Financial Advisor

This service is intended for companies. I give advice to improve your profits, lower operating costs, financial audits, definition of an investment strategy, market studies.


These services are independent of each other, you can subscribe to one or more of these services. The idea is to offer a personalized service adapted to each situation, taking into account the specificity of the company, the sector of activity, the economic situation (micro and macroeconomic), local demand.

Ethics charter

I promise not to invest your money in companies that employ minors under the age of 14, using resources from an armed conflict, do not respect Human Rights, women's rights, finance criminal organizations or do not transparent.


I prioritize companies that promote gender equality, transparency, reliability in commitments, respect the law, long-term vision, good management of resources.
